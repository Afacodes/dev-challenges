All About Breads

Breads around the world

One of the delicacies savoured all over the world is bread. How a sticky ball of dough can create such wonderful textures and flavours is absolutely stunning. Bread can be made using different kinds of flour and ingredients in a variety of ways. The three main kinds of bread are:

  • Those that have a high rise.
  • Those with medium volume.
  • Those that hardly rise, called flatbreads.

The additions you can make to your breads are spices, fruits, vegetables, nuts and fragrant herbs.
